❓ GRF Treasury FX — Help & User Guide
What is this tool?
The GRF Treasury FX system is a unified treasury tool for monitoring EUR/USD and USD/ZAR exchange rates, evaluating bank deal rates against market rates, measuring pip margins, generating deal confirmation emails, and viewing full analytics — all in one file.
Two Tabs
Deal Entry: Paste rates, enter bank quote, check pip margin, confirm deal & copy email.
Dashboard: Full analytics, charts, and deal history with edit & delete.
Tip: Keep FXStreet open in one browser tab and this tool in another. All data is saved automatically in your browser.
Step-by-Step Guide
1
Select Currency Pair
Choose EUR/USD or USD/ZAR using the pair tabs at the top of Deal Entry.
2
Add Market Rate
Copy the current rate from FXStreet, paste it into the rate field, click Add Rate. Repeat as the rate moves to build a version history.
3
Select Deal Type & Enter Details
Choose Sell EUR / Buy EUR / Sell USD / Buy USD. Enter the bank deal rate, amount, deal number, value date, and who gave the rate.
4
Read Pip Analysis
The pip margin, colour-coded verdict, and scale marker update instantly. See the Pip Scale tab for what each zone means.
5
Confirm & Copy Email
Click Confirm Deal to generate the email. Click Copy to Clipboard then paste into Outlook.
6
View Analytics
Switch to the Dashboard tab to see charts, KPIs, and the full deal table. Edit or delete any deal directly from the table.
Pip Margin Scale
Pip margin = |bank rate − market spot rate| × 10,000. Lower is always better for the company.
ZoneRangeMeaningAction
⭐ Impressive0–4 pipsVery competitive rateConfirm immediately
✅ Satisfactory5–7 pipsWithin acceptable rangeSafe to confirm
⚠ Concerning7–8 pipsAbove policy limitAsk bank to tighten
🔸 High Margin8–9 pipsWell above limitNegotiate firmly
❌ Red Flag>9 pipsUnacceptableReject & request new quote
Policy: Maximum allowed bank margin is 7 pips. Below 5 pips is a win for the company.

Rate Version Tracker
Each time you paste a rate and click Add Rate, it is logged with a timestamp. After two or more entries, the system analyses the trend and gives a plain-English suggestion based on your deal type — telling you whether the rate movement is working for or against you.
Session High/Low shows the best and worst rate you have seen this session, so you can judge if the current rate is near the top or bottom of today's range.
History, Edit & Delete
Every confirmed deal is automatically saved to your browser's local storage and persists across sessions. From the Dashboard tab you can:
✎
Edit a Deal
Click the blue Edit button on any row. A modal opens with all deal fields pre-filled. Change what you need and click Save.
🗑
Delete a Deal
Click the red Delete button on any row. You will be asked to confirm before the record is permanently removed.
You can also Export CSV from the Deal Entry tab to download all data, or Clear All to wipe history.
Note: Clearing your browser cookies or site data will erase history. Export CSV regularly as a backup.
